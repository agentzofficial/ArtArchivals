The Sploosh-o-matic and Agent Z go way back -- being one of the first weapons (besides Marie's rocket launcher and the Hero Shot...) to appear on the agent z channel as a whole, in the second video animation on the channel!

Wow, only 2 years ago. Crazy to see how much the animations have improved since! ...At least, I think they improved? And whoops, you can clearly see that the weapons haven't had their colors altered to match their team colors, like the Sploosh-o-matic being a default dull green instead of purple.

Since it first appeared on the channel, the Sploosh-o-matic became an icon of its own, frequently appearing alongside Agent Z in videos, banners, and even ingame! Unless it was a new season with new weapons, it was be pretty difficult to find Agent Z in games with any weapon but the Sploosh-o-matic! Playing the weapon almost exclusively, she has gained a total of... how many wins with the weapon?!

But enough from me, what does Agent Z and her friend, Agent X have to say about this?

Agent Z
I LOVE the Sploosh-o-Matic! I can zip-zap around the map and splat everybody!
Agent X
...Or you can just run up against the enemy team alone and get yourself killed immediately.
Agent Z
Ehh?! What are you talking about, I don't do that!
Agent X
Literally every Sploosh does it. You run up, die, and spam "Ouch!" as if we can do anything about it.
Agent Z
Well... when that strategy DOES work, you have to admit that it's a pretty strong opener for the match!
Agent X
So is using weapons with ANY sort of range at all, which will also lower your chances of dying instantly.
Agent Z
Pfft! Where's the fun in that!? I can just toss a Curling Bomb and get right in on the action!
Agent X
A Curling Bomb... could you be ANY more predictable? Do you even WANT to win your games?
Agent Z
Well... as long as I'm having fun, I consider it a win!
Agent X
I'm sure your teammates would like to have a word with you about that.
