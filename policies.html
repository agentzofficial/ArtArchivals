Usage Policies

This page is dedicated to detailing how agent z content, including their renders, videos, characters, or other forms of media, may be used for your own content. Thank you for your interest.

In general, it is only asked that agent z's content is respected and not used for malicious purposes. If you're unsure and wish for specifics, please continue reading below.

agent z Art Archivals Content

All images published onto the website by agent z or on official social platforms may be freely used for anything you like. Credit is optional but appreciated. To respectfully use agent z content, please:

  • Do not use or repost agent z content with the intention of plagarism or impersonation.
  • Do not falsely claim that you are directly affiliated with the content, such as (but not limited to) claiming the content was made in a collaboration with agent z if it was not.
  • Do not alter content, including adding captions, to portray a potentially controversial or serious topic without explicility stating that it is edited and not officially endorsed by agent z.

agent z Video Content

The guidelines for the agent z Art Archivals Content above all also apply to the use of agent z video content. In addition, please ensure you:

  • Expand the video using your own creative input, such as with meaningful commentary, live reactions, or significant enough editing that differs the viewing experience entirely. This particularly applies if a significant portion of the video is being used.

agent z Characters

Original characters introduced by agent z, being Agent Z and Agent X, may be used or represented in your own content or fan artwork. However, you are NOT to:

  • Maliciously misrepresent the characters such as (not limited to) with their personality or appearance.
  • Use the characters in any political or other sensitive context.
  • Falsely claim you are affiliated with agent z if you are not.

Thank you for taking the time to reading these policies. If you have any queries or wish to report potential copyright infringement, please contact me through the Contact page.
